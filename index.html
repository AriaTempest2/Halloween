Horror Mario

M, also know as Ultra M, is a horrorbrew created by Marco Antonio, and is the main antagonist encountered in Mario's Madness. The character itself is a take on two separate horrorbrews by combining them into one: Shadow-Shana's "Super Horror Mario" artwork and Coolrash's Mario.exe from the eponymous game.

GB

GB is a Game Boy inspired horrorbrew created by Marco Antonio. He is based off of Mario's appearance in Super Mario Land 2: 6 Golden Coins, and is specifically from a bootleg copy of the game, with his origins having been unknown for awhile. He likes to play with the victims who dare play his game. He can also manifest in his players head and come into the real world. He mostly chooses not to, however, as he believes limiting himself makes things more fun.

MX

MX is a horrorbrew owned by @RazzDazzleDoo. He is the main antagonist in the Creepypasta game Mario '85, which is meant to be a love letter to the original Super Mario Bros. game. He's an unknown entity who inhabited a copy of Super Mario Bros. and disguised himself as a slightly-off version of Mario in order to lure unsuspecting players/victims into the game and play with for all eternity. He is Lucas's tormentor, of whom he killed and pulled his soul into his digital hellish dimension, forcing him into the body of Luigi.

The Wario Apparition is a character created by Nintendo and popularized by the Mario 64 horror community with a creepypasta based on the theory that every copy of the original Super Mario 64 is personalized, and that there exists a cursed beta version of the game from July 29, 1995.
